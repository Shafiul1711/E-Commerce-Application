How to Place an Order

Ordering on Puzzlemania is easy! Just follow the instructions.

  1. Visit the Products page to browse available puzzles.
  2. Click Add to Cart on any puzzle you'd like to purchase.
  3. After selecting your puzzles, go to the Cart page to review your order.
  4. Click on Proceed to Checkout.
  5. Fill out the required checkout details and click Confirm Order.

Once your order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation on-screen. And an confirmation email will be sent to your account
